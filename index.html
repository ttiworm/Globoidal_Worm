小型环面包络蜗杆的新型加工方法

A New Machining Method for Small Globoidal Enveloping Worms

1. 加工方法简介

1. Introduction

环面包络蜗轮蜗杆传动的加工方法目前主要有以下几种方式:

Currently, there are several machining methods for globoidal enveloping worm gear drives:

1) 车床切削法(包括普通车床和圆弧车床),该种方法加工速度较慢,精度较低,而且只能加工大型(中心距40以上)的蜗杆;

1) Lathe cutting method (including ordinary lathes and circular arc lathes). This method is slow, has low precision, and can only process large worms (center distance over 40).

2) 使用专用磨床加工,该技术方案同样也是针对中心距比较大的蜗杆加工方式,成本较高,效率较低，中心距越小精度越低。

2) Special grinding machine processing. This solution is also for larger worms. It has high cost, low efficiency, and precision decreases as center distance gets smaller.

-- 本技术文章主要要解决了小型/微型环面包络的蜗杆加工的精度 and 效率问题.目前的环面蜗杆加工技术,主要都是针对中心距比较大的蜗杆加工,使用同样的设备和工具加工小中心距(中心距30mm以下),这些加工方案都是难以加工出理想的产品,加工效率也是非常低下,成本过高.而且目前市场上没有其他可以完美替代的方案。

-- This technical article mainly solves the accuracy and efficiency issues of small/micro globoidal enveloping worm machining. Existing technologies target large center distances. Using the same equipment for small distances (under 30mm) makes it difficult to achieve ideal products, results in low efficiency, and high costs. Currently, there is no other perfect alternative on the market.

2. Working Principle

Working principle is as follows:

This machining technology mainly involves globoidal worms that can be mated with helical gears (including involute, straight, circular arc, cycloid teeth, etc., as shown from left to right in Figure 01).

这种蜗杆可以和直齿轮做配合传动,也可以和斜齿轮做配合传动,本说明以直齿齿形作为展示.蜗杆齿轮传动示意图如图02所示:

This type of worm can be used for transmission with both spur gears and helical gears. This description uses a spur gear profile for demonstration. The worm-gear transmission diagram is shown in Figure 02:

图02 环面蜗杆和斜齿轮传动方式 Fig. 02 Transmission mode of globoidal worm and helical gear

蜗杆和斜齿轮的速比是\(i12\), 中心距为a,斜齿轮的基圆螺旋角为β,基圆直径为d.

The speed ratio of the worm and helical gear is \(i12\), the center distance is a, the base circle helix angle of the helical gear is β, and the base circle diameter is d.

蜗杆的加工是以斜齿轮的模型端面轮廓作为驱动线做一把车齿刀具,其简图如图03所示.蜗杆车齿刀和以蜗杆配合的斜齿轮端面作为驱动面.蜗杆车齿刀的驱动面在直径方向的齿需要稍稍加厚以作避空,一般单边加厚0.2*m(m为齿轮的模数).

The machining of the worm uses the end-face profile of the helical gear model as a driving line to create a skiving cutter, as shown in the diagram in Figure 03. The driving surface of the worm skiving cutter is based on the end face of the helical gear that mates with the worm. The teeth on the driving surface of the worm skiving cutter need to be slightly thickened in the diameter direction for clearance, generally by 0.2*m on each side (where m is the gear module).

3. 蜗杆车齿刀具体形状

3. Skiving Cutter Types

蜗杆车齿刀具体形状有几种,具体如下:

There are several specific shapes for worm skiving cutters, as follows:

1、蜗杆车齿刀形状和斜齿轮的形状一致(端面截面、螺旋角等参数),然后和刀柄做成一体,实际的加工中需要做加上整体后角用来做切削避空,避空的没有要求一定要和螺旋线的参数一致.只需要刀具的非切削部位比斜齿轮的圆周齿面低,实际的加工过程中刀具后角位置不会和加工的环面蜗杆发生干涉.,如图05所示。

1. The shape of the worm skiving cutter matches the helical gear (end-face section, helix angle, etc.) and is integrated with the tool shank. In actual machining, an overall clearance angle must be added for cutting clearance; the clearance doesn't need to match the helix parameters exactly. It only requires that the non-cutting parts of the tool are lower than the helical tooth surface of the gear. During actual machining, the clearance angle position will not interfere with the processed globoidal worm, as shown in Figure 05.

图05 蜗杆车齿刀种类1 Fig. 05 Worm skiving cutter Type 1

2、蜗杆车齿刀端面和斜齿轮端面一致,但是不做螺旋角（或者螺旋角为0）,然后和刀柄做成一体,在实际的加工中需要加上整体后角用来做切削避空,整体后角需要保证在实际加工过程中不会碰到加工的环面蜗杆,如图04所示.

2. The end face of the worm skiving cutter matches the helical gear end face, but has no helix angle (or the helix angle is 0). It is integrated with the tool shank. In actual machining, an overall clearance angle must be added for cutting clearance. The clearance angle must ensure no contact with the globoidal worm during machining, as shown in Figure 06.

图06 蜗杆车齿刀种类2 Fig. 06 Worm skiving cutter Type 2

3、以上两种方案,刀柄可以根蜗杆车齿刀刀头(切削位置)做成分体,如图07和图08所示。

3. In the above two solutions, the tool shank can be made separate from the worm skiving cutter head (cutting position), as shown in Figures 07 and 08.

图07 种类3 分体式蜗杆车齿刀 Fig. 07 Type 3 Segmented worm skiving cutter

图08 种类4 分体式蜗杆车齿刀 Fig. 08 Type 4 Segmented worm skiving cutter

分体式蜗杆车齿刀爆炸图如下,使用螺母对车刀片进行压紧（在螺母和车齿刀片之间可以加垫片）,这样刀具的成本可以大幅度降低，但是精度有所降低，如图09所示。

The exploded view of the segmented worm skiving cutter is as follows. A nut is used to clamp the cutter blade (spacers can be added between the nut and the cutter blade). This significantly reduces tool costs, though precision is slightly lower, as shown in Figure 09.

图09 分体式蜗杆车齿刀爆炸图 Fig. 09 Exploded view of segmented worm skiving cutter

The worm skiving cutter can be designed with a rake angle depending on actual conditions, generally in two ways:

蜗杆车齿刀螺旋角比较小或者为0度的时候,刀具前角可以设计成图10所示,刀具前角位置直接做成大圆弧面,这样利于刀具切削。

When the helix angle of the worm skiving cutter is small or zero, the rake angle can be designed as shown in Figure 10. The rake angle position is directly made into a large arc surface, which facilitates cutting.

图10 前角设计01 Fig. 10 Rake angle design 01

前角也可以设计成斜边，如图11所示。

The rake angle can also be designed as a bevel, as shown in Figure 11.

图11 前角设计02 Fig. 11 Rake angle design 02

蜗杆车齿刀螺旋角比较大的时候,前角可以设计成阶梯前角,如图12所示:

When the helix angle of the worm skiving cutter is large, the rake angle can be designed as a stepped rake angle, as shown in Figure 12:

图12 前角设计03 Fig. 12 Rake angle design 03

条件不允许的情况下,也可以不设计前角（前角设计02中的前角为0度）,这样刀具也可以加工,只不是效果差一点.

If conditions do not allow, the rake angle can be omitted (rake angle is 0 degrees in Design 02). The tool can still process, but with slightly reduced performance.

Note: It is necessary to ensure that the projected end-face section of the worm skiving cutter after setting the rake angle is consistent with the designed section.

4. 加工方式

4. Machining Methods

蜗杆车齿刀可以根据实际情况做全齿车齿刀，和非全齿车齿刀。全齿车齿刀刀具齿数和斜齿轮的齿数完全一致，非全齿车齿刀可以根据刀具的实际加工情况适当减少端面齿数，如图13所示：

Worm skiving cutters can be full-tooth or non-full-tooth depending on actual conditions. A full-tooth cutter has the same number of teeth as the helical gear. A non-full-tooth cutter can have fewer teeth depending on processing requirements, as shown in Figure 13:

图13 全齿蜗杆车齿刀和少齿蜗杆车齿刀 Fig. 13 Full-tooth vs. Fewer-tooth worm skiving cutters

蜗杆的加工方式如图14所示:

The worm machining method is shown in Figure 14:

图14 蜗杆车齿刀加工蜗杆图示 Fig. 14 Schematic of worm machining with skiving cutter

因为蜗杆车齿刀端面截面是完全和斜齿轮的端面一致,所以这一种加工方式可以这样表示:

Since the end-face section of the worm skiving cutter is identical to that of the helical gear, this machining method can be described as follows:

蜗杆轴和刀具轴分别进入角度模式,角度初始位置均设置为0,

The worm axis and tool axis enter angle mode respectively, with initial positions both set to 0.

刀具的初始位置如下图所示其中初始X1要比蜗杆最大外圆半径大一点,初始值Y1位置大于中心距加上蜗杆齿高.如图15所示

The initial position of the tool is shown below. The initial X1 should be slightly larger than the maximum outer radius of the worm, and the initial Y1 should be greater than the center distance plus the worm tooth height, as shown in Figure 15.

图15 蜗杆车齿刀加工初始位置侧视图 Fig. 15 Side view of initial machining position

车齿刀Z方向的中心位置就是蜗杆的中心位置,如途中的初始Z1,如图16所示

The center position of the skiving cutter in the Z direction is the center of the worm, as shown by Z1 in Figure 16.

图16 蜗杆车齿刀加工俯视图 Fig. 16 Top view of worm skiving

蜗杆车齿刀具体的加工方法如图17所示.刀具先从初始点走Y方向到第一刀的下刀点,然后走X方向从X1加工到-X1,然后Y方向退刀,然后X方向退刀到初始位置.这样形成一个加工循环.根据这个循环加工第二刀,第三刀,一直到最后一刀.最后一刀加工完成后.再经由Y方向和X方向退刀到初始位置.最后一刀加工时,车齿刀中心和蜗杆中心的距离应为中心距a（理想状态，可根据实际情况做修改）。

The specific machining method is shown in Figure 17. The tool moves from the starting point along the Y direction to the first cutting depth, then moves along the X direction from X1 to -X1, retracts in the Y direction, and then returns to the starting position in the X direction. This forms one machining cycle. Subsequent cuts follow this cycle until the final cut. After the final cut, the tool retracts in Y and returns to the initial X. During the final cut, the distance between the cutter center and worm center should be the center distance 'a' (ideal state, adjustable).

图17 车齿刀的走刀方式 Fig. 17 Tool path of the skiving cutter

5. 数学模型与参数关系

5. Mathematical Model and Parameters

在加工的过程中,在初始位置和下刀点的位置车刀和蜗杆的角度均为0,车齿刀加工的过程中，蜗杆和刀具遵循一定的角度关系。在刀具每刀加工完成后（Y方向退刀前）。蜗杆的旋转角度为C1, 车齿刀的旋转角度C2为:

During machining, at the initial position and the start point, the angles of the cutter and worm are both 0. During machining, the worm and tool follow a specific angular relationship. After each cut (before retracting in Y), if the worm's rotation angle is C1, then the cutter's rotation angle C2 is:

\[C2=±[C1/i12+π∗d∗tanβ/(2∗X1)];\]

2*X1是蜗杆车齿刀加工蜗杆X方向的长度.

2*X1 is the travel length of the worm skiving cutter in the X direction.

d为斜齿轮基圆直径.

d is the base circle diameter of the helical gear.

β为基圆螺旋角.

β is the base circle helix angle.

i12为减速机系统的减速比.

i12 is the reduction ratio of the system.

上面正负对应的是螺旋斜齿的左旋或者右旋.

The plus/minus signs correspond to left-hand or right-hand helical teeth.

Y方向退刀的过程中,蜗杆和车齿刀也应该同步转起来,蜗杆的角度是C1+C3,车齿刀的角度是C4为:

During retraction in the Y direction, the worm and cutter should also rotate synchronously. If the worm's angle is C1+C3, then the cutter's angle C4 is:

\[C4=C2±C3/i12\]

同理上面正负对应的是螺旋斜齿的左旋或者右旋.

Similarly, the plus/minus signs correspond to left-hand or right-hand helical teeth.

X方向退刀完成后蜗杆和车齿刀的角度都要分别归0,然后走到第二刀下刀点,再做下一个角度同步加工.如此往复,直到完成最后一步加工.经由Y方向和X方向回到初始点.至此蜗杆加工完成.

After the X-direction retraction is complete, both the worm and cutter angles are reset to 0, and they move to the next cutting point for another synchronous cycle. This repeats until machining is finished. The tool then returns to the starting point. The worm machining is now complete.

因为Y方向每次退刀不一致，所以每次的退刀的C3和C4变量值也可以根据实际情况不一样。同时在实际加工过程中需要综合计算车齿刀分度圆位置的线速度，符合机加工的要求。

Since the Y-direction retraction distance varies, variables C3 and C4 can also vary. Additionally, the linear velocity at the cutter's pitch circle should be calculated to meet machining requirements.

在刀具加工的过加工有两个特例,分别如下:

There are two special cases during machining:

1、齿轮不是斜齿,而是直齿的时候（β值为0）,蜗杆和车齿刀的角度关系如下

1. When the gear is a spur gear (β = 0), the angular relationship is:

\[C2=±C1/i12\]

这时候,只需要使用种类2或者种类4的蜗杆车齿刀

In this case, only Type 2 or Type 4 worm skiving cutters are needed.

2、某些机床刀具主轴没有角度模式,也可以使用角速度模式进行加工。

2. If some machine tool spindles do not have an angle mode, angular velocity mode can also be used.

这时候需要保证车齿刀的转速是蜗杆转速除以速比,并尽可能让两个转速都是整数.

Ensure that the cutter speed is the worm speed divided by the ratio, aiming for integer speeds if possible.

\[V2=±V1/i12;\]

其中V1是蜗杆的转速,V2是蜗杆车齿刀的转速。

where V1 is the worm speed and V2 is the cutter speed.

当齿轮是直齿的时候,也可以用第二种方法来做,当斜齿的基圆螺旋角度不是很大的时候(5度以内),使用第第一种和第二种方法都可以,但是斜齿和蜗杆在实际的组装过程中需要跑合才能使用。

For spur gears, the second method can also be used. When the base circle helix angle is small (within 5 degrees), either method works, but the helical gear and worm may need running-in during assembly.
